## Client Side

Team members are viewed at multiple locations on your site.

Including: About, prequalify, prequalification, etc..

### Viewing Team

Navigate to `/admin/team`.

On this page you will find a table with the attributes listed for **Team**

![Viewing Team Image](/docs/team/view.png)

### Creating Team

Locate the "Create" Button, at the **Top Right of the Table Header**.

![Create Resource Image](/docs/team/create.png)

This will open up a form, allowing you to fill in all information. Please refer to the attributes table for what each of these fields represents.

![Create Form Resource Image](/docs/team/createForm.png)

### Editing Team

Every row entry will have an **Edit** Button. Press this to open up the editing form.

![Edit Resource Image](/docs/team/edit.png)
